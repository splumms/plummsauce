About

A little bit about me... I'm a Toronto based web developer with a "really good" coffee addiction and a love for animals (particularly dogs and horses). I went to school for Animal Biology specializing in behaviour and nutrition, but when the pandemic hit and I found myself with a lot of time on my hands I decided I'd learn how to code. Fast forward 2 years and I'm now working full time as a web-developer.

If you're surprised by the above switch in fields, you're not the only one. If you had asked me 3 years ago what I would be doing now, I would never have guessed this...and while I would never go so far as to say I'm grateful for the pandemic I can say that in the long run it changed my life for the better. Was that too cheesy? Probably, but you've now gotten to know me a little better.
